Unveiling the Secrets of Object Detection

Ever wondered how our platform identifies objects so accurately?
It starts with your uploaded data – images, videos, or live camera feeds. Our system processes this input to prepare it for detection.

At the core of our technology is the powerful You Only Look Once (YOLOv8) model. This advanced AI processes the entire image in one go, breaking it into a grid. Each grid cell predicts bounding boxes and class probabilities for objects within that cell. This allows the model to quickly and accurately find objects, label them, and draw boxes around them.
We refine these detections using a technique called non-maximum suppression (NMS). This ensures we get the most accurate results by eliminating duplicate detections.

Our system is always learning. With just a few new pictures, it can recognize new types of objects. This continuous improvement means our platform remains top-notch, offering real-time, cross-device object detection that is both simple and smart.
